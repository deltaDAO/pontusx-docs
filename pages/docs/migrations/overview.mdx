# Overview: Migration Guides for Pontus-X

Migrating from testnet to mainnet is a crucial step in deploying your assets on a live network. This chapter provides detailed guidance on best practices, verification steps, and frequently asked questions to ensure a smooth transition.

## Why Migration Matters

Migration from testnet to mainnet ensures that your applications and assets are fully functional in a real-world environment. It enables actual transactions and interactions with other users in a secure and decentralized setting.

**Key Benefits of Migration**
- Monetization: Enable real transactions and economic incentives.
- Reliability: Deploy assets in a stable environment with real economic value.
- Interoperability: Engage with other mainnet participants and services.
