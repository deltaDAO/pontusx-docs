# The EU Data Act 

## Strategic assessment: opportunities  for European businesses 
An analysis of the intersection between Data Act regulation and Pontus-X technology reveals a nuanced picture for European businesses. 


### Opportunities

-**Data sovereignty as a service** By using C2D, an SME can grant data access (Data Act compliance) without leaking IP. Resolves the main dilemma of the Data Act (transparency vs. confidentiality).

-**New sources of revenue** Data that was previously a ‘by-product’ of production can be sold via the marketplace (e.g. quality data to researchers). Transformation from a pure manufacturer to a service provider.

-**Efficiency through interoperability** Access to data from upstream suppliers enables better planning (resilience) and automatic optimisation of one’s own production. Cost reduction and risk minimisation in the supply chain.
