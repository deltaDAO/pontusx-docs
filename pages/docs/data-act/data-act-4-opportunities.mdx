# The EU Data Act 

## Strategic assessment: opportunities  for European businesses 
An analysis of the intersection between Data Act regulation and Pontus-X technology reveals strategic opportunities for European businesses. 


### Opportunities

- **Data sovereignty as a service.** By using C2D, an SME can grant data access (Data Act compliance) without leaking IP. Resolves the main dilemma of the Data Act (transparency vs. confidentiality).

- **New sources of revenue.** Data that was previously a ‘by-product’ of production can be sold via marketplaces. Businesses transform from a pure manufacturer to a service provider.

- **Efficiency through interoperability.** Access to data from upstream suppliers enables better planning, resilience, and automatic optimisation of one’s own production. This enables cost reduction and risk minimisation in the supply chain.
